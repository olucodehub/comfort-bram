About Our Menu

Our wedding menu celebrates the beautiful fusion of Nigerian and Dutch cultures. The Nigerian dishes represent Comfort's heritage and the flavors she grew up with, while the international options ensure all our guests can enjoy the celebration. Each dish is prepared with authentic recipes and the finest ingredients.

What is Asoebi?

Asoebi is a beautiful Nigerian tradition where family and friends wear matching or coordinated outfits to show unity and support for the couple. You'll see many of our guests wearing matching fabrics and colors - this is our way of celebrating together as one big family!
